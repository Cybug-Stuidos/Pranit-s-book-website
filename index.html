Zach King -

11-year-old Zach has magical power... he just hasn't found them yet. But when he starts at a regular secondary school with 'normal' children, his powers are unlocked and bit of magic leads to him going viral on YouTube. It attracts the attenion of Rachel, the loveliest girl at school, but with everyone wondering how he does his tricks, and mean girl Tricia plotting a major prank to bring him down, Zach's got his work cut out to keep his secret and survive Year Seven!

Artemis Fowl -

No scheme is too dastardly, no plot too devious. And he's just discovered that fairies are real. Poor fairies. But these are not the gentle creatures of bedtime stories. They are armed.They are dangerous. Then Artemis kidnaps elf Captain Holly Short in an attempt to ransom her for fairy gold. Holly is incredibly dangerous, and pretty annoyed about that whole kidnapping thing. Artemis Fowl is about to find out that fairies fight back...
